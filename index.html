Welcome to our "Oh Wow Ceramics!" GitHub page!

The Fantastic-journey project was created for a company called "Oh Wow Ceramics!" It helps users find shops and artists with ceramics in their area. The project uses the Etsy API.

Authors and Contributors

In May of 2016, CJ Reyes (@itscjreyes), Dana Centeno (@danacenteno), and David Dales (@davidd647) created the Oh Wow Ceramics: Fantastic-Journey project.
